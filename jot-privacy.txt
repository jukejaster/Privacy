Privacy Policy
This policy applies to the information inside of Jot.

Information we collect
Any information inputted into Jot is not collected by justncode LLC in any way, but it is stored locally on your device to keep track of the reminders you've set for yourself. If you opt into receiving local notifications so you are reminded when you need to complete tasks, we'll store the date of when you want to be reminded. If you decide to delete the reminder for a task, we'll then remove the reminder date from your device.

Your Consent
By using Jot, you consent to our privacy policy.
