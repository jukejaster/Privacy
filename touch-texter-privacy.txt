PRIVACY POLICY

We take your privacy as serious as we take our own. Check out everything we're doing to protect your information.

This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use Speed Typer.


PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT
We share your high scores with the Game Center leaderboards upon your authorization. Any other information is not collected by justncode LLC in any way. In other words, your high score is stored locally on your device to keep track of your progress in regards to your texting speed. By using Speed Typer, you consent to our privacy policy.

GOOGLE ANALYTICS
We use Google Analytics to collect usage and behavior data for Speed Typer. This data could be anything such as user actions, system events, or errors. These analytics help us understand how our users use the app. Ultimately, these analytics help us understand how our users behave, so that we can make informed decisions about to make the product better.

GOOGLE CRASHLYTICS
We strive to make Speed Typer as stable as possible. Therefore, we leverage a crash reporter by Google named Crashlytics. This crash reporter will let us know of any crashes that are experienced inside of the app so that we can fix them.

PERSONALIZED ADVERTISING
Speed Typer leverages Google's AdMob to deliver personalized advertisements to you so that we can keep the application running for free. Since Speed Typer uses AdMob, we collect, share and use any user data as a result to help devlier these advertisements.
